3️⃣ State Minimum Liability & Extras

✅ State Minimum Liability
Bodily Injury (BI): $30,000 per person / $60,000 per accident
Property Damage (PD): $15,000 per accident
These are the legal minimums required in this state.
⚠️ Strongly Recommended: Supplemental Liability (SLI)
$100,000 per person / $300,000 per accident / $100,000 property damage
Extra protection beyond state minimums, especially for business travel.
⚠️ Recommended: UM/UIM Coverage
$100,000 per person / $300,000 per accident
Covers you if the other driver doesn’t have enough insurance.
